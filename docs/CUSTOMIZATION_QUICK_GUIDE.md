## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### 1. Progressive Learning Path
```
Week 1-2: Everything ON
         â†“
Week 3-4: Start hiding "Did You Know"
         â†“
Week 5-6: Hide "Exploratory" too
         â†“
Week 7-8: Practice with Hints OFF
         â†“
Week 9+:  Exam mode (all OFF)
```

### 2. Subject-Specific Strategies

**Math/Physics**
- Keep: Shortcuts, If-Then Scenarios, Quick Recall
- Hide: Did You Know, Exploratory

**Conceptual Subjects**
- Keep: Key Insights, Exploratory, Common Mistakes
- Hide: Shortcuts (less relevant)

**Mixed Problems**
- Keep: Everything initially
- Adjust based on comfort level

### 3. Time Management

**Short on Time?**
- Hide: Did You Know, Exploratory, Pro Tips
- Keep: Core solution, Shortcuts, Quick Recall

**Plenty of Time?**
- Enable everything for deep understanding

## â“ FAQ

**Q: Will my settings affect other students?**  
A: No, settings are personal and saved only on your device.

**Q: Do I need to save settings?**  
A: No, they save automatically when you toggle.

**Q: Can I reset if I mess up?**  
A: Yes, click "Reset to Default" in the settings modal.

**Q: Why can't I see some features?**  
A: Strategy & Common Mistakes require a premium subscription.

**Q: Do settings work offline?**  
A: Yes, they're stored locally on your device.

**Q: Can I use different settings for different subjects?**  
A: Not yet, but it's planned for a future update!
